About Me:

I am an undergraduate student pursuing a dual major in Culture, Literature & the Arts and Media & Communication Studies at the University of Washington Bothell, alongside a minor in Computer Science. I'm also a freelance photographer and musician, a hobbyist poet and programmer, and a full-time nerd. Some of my academic interests include fan culture and Internet culture, critical game studies, digital humanities, science fiction and technoculture, and 20th-21st century American literature.


I have always been passionate about literature, technology, education, and the blending of different cultures, which led to me pursuing a scholarly or academic career now. I have worked as a teaching assistant at UWB, a research assistant at the Center on Reinventing Public Education [CRPE], and will be working as a peer advisor for first-year students at UWB next year. I have had scholarly and creative work published in The CROW, Clamor, and The Bellevue Bastion.


Feel free to look around this site if any of that interests you! If you wanna chat, feel free to email me!
